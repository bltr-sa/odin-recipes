Chinese Mapo Tofu

Description

A simple, delicious Chinese mapo tofu recipe (mabo dofu is the Japanese adaptation) that has been passed on for years.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 5 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon hot bean sauce
  • 1/2 pound ground pork

Steps

  1. Brown ground pork in a small skillet over medium-high heat, about 5 mintues. Drain fat.
  2. Heat oil over medium-high heat in a large skillet or wok. Cook garlic and ginger in oil until fragrant, about 1 minute. Stir in tofu and cook for 2 mintues. Season with soy sauce, hot bean sauce; stir to combine.
  3. Stir in cooked pork and green onions. Stir sesame oil into the mapo tofu.
